About the Event

The First Emirati Products Week in Rwanda brings together top-tier manufacturers from the U.A.E. to showcase innovation, craftsmanship, and commercial excellence. Join us from October 26 to 28, 2025, at the Kigali Convention Center to experience the future of Emirati trade and culture.

Our Mission

To empower Emirati manufacturers by creating a global platform that promotes innovation, trade, and cultural exchange between the U.A.E. and Rwanda through impactful exhibitions and partnerships.

Our Vision

To establish the First Emirati Products Week as a landmark event that strengthens international ties, celebrates excellence in Emirati production, and inspires economic collaboration across borders.
